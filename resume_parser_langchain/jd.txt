Job Description: AI / Networking Teaching Assistant

We are hiring a candidate with strong technical ability in TCP/IP, AI, and RAG, plus the ability to function as a teaching assistant in a practical, hands-on environment.

Responsibilities

- Support work involving TCP/IP networking, socket communication, basic protocol understanding, and network troubleshooting.
- Assist with the development and testing of AI / LLM-based applications.
- Help build and improve RAG systems, including document processing, embeddings, retrieval, and response generation.
- Provide teaching assistant support, including answering questions, explaining technical concepts, supporting labs or exercises, and helping others debug problems.
- Contribute to technical documentation, demos, and implementation tasks.

Requirements

- Solid understanding of TCP/IP and network fundamentals.
- Practical experience with AI / LLM systems.
- Familiarity with RAG pipelines and related workflows.
- Ability to explain technical material clearly and support others in a teaching or mentoring capacity.
- Strong hands-on technical and coding skills.

Candidate Screening

We care about technical competence, execution, and teaching ability.

If your profile is heavily centered on club activities, student organizations, or similar extracurricular involvement, you are not a fit for this role. We do not consider such experience valuable here, and candidates who emphasize it will be rejected.

Application Note

Please focus your application on:

- TCP/IP or networking experience
- AI / LLM / RAG experience
- Teaching assistant, tutoring, or mentoring experience
- Strong technical projects.
